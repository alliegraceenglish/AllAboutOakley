This is a site all about Oakley the Beaglier!

Oakley is a 5 year old black and tan beaglier - a Beagle and Cavlier King Charles mix. She was outside of Nashville, Tennessee, and came to be a part of her forever home in 2013. Oakley is funny, snuggly, and super cute. The more you know her, the more you're sure to love her!

About Oakley

Oakley is the best Beaglier ever! She is a five-year-old black and tan Beaglier who loves people. Oakley is very friendly and loves other dogs and people. She loves snuggling more than almost anything, and she is always down to cuddle. Oakley is also a big fan of food and treats, epecially ice! Overall, Oakley has a wonderful temperment and is truly one of the best friends anyone could have.

Hobbies

Oakley has a variety of hobbies. She loves to chew bones, play fetch, and get involved in an intense game of tug-of-war. When it comes to playing fetch, her red squeaky ball is her favorite, but her ropes are perfect for playing tug-of-war. Oakley also loves sleeping. She can nap just about anywhere whether it's the master bed, the guest bed, her doggie bed, or even the floor. Oakley enjoys going for walks where she can meet other dogs and people. When the weather is warm she enjoys going to the dog park, but she's usually more interested in the people rather than the other dogs. She is always so excited when she can get pets from new friends!

A Typical Day

A typical day for Oakley begins with a trip outside. While she loves to smell and explore, she knows breakfast is her next stop, so she usually gets down to taking care of business pretty quickly. After her outdoor excursion, Oakley helps herself to a big bowl of her favorite dog food. After breakfast, Oakley typically enjoys a nice long drink and does some exploring around the house. Once she's satisfied everything is safe and sound, Oakley might go back to sleep on her special blanket if her humans are still around.

Sometimes Oakley spends the day at her favorite doggie day care where she gets to play with all her puppy and people friends.

Her People

Oakley's people are Mason and Allie English. She thinks they're pretty great! Mason and Allie got Oakley when they were in school, and the day they got married and the whole family moved in together was one of Oakley's favorites. Right now Mason is in school to be a doctor. He studies a lot and works really hard. Allie has a neat job and is learning all about business. They really love Oakley, and she really loves them too.
